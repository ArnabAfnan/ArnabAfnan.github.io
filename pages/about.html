Hello, I'm Afnan

Building Retro Canvas,
frame by frame

Retro Canvas started in 2022 with one camera, one couple, and a simple belief — that South Asian weddings deserve to be documented exactly as they feel: loud, colourful, layered with ritual, and full of people who love you.

Since then we've grown into a small, dedicated team based in Dhaka, working across Bangladesh and beyond. Photographers, cinematographers, editors — every person on this team is here because they care about getting it right, not just getting it done.

When I'm not on set, I'm probably planning the next wedding, backing up hard drives late at night, or arguing with the team about which take made the final cut.

Afnan Rahman Arnab
