Terms & Conditions

  • All the packages are customizable according to your preferences.
  • For outside Dhaka, additional transportation & accommodation costs with 25% extra of the package will be added.
  • Core Photographer packages are available only for locations within Dhaka.
  • Any combo package can be customized from the packages listed above.
  • For event booking confirmation, 50% advance payment is required. The remaining balance is due at the time of raw copies delivery (within two days of the last event).
  • Final delivery: within two to four weeks.
  • For Pre & Post-Wedding shoots, 100% advance payment is required at the time of booking confirmation.
  • Within one week of the event, the client must send song selections for video editing.
  • If you prefer not to have your photos & videos posted on social media, an additional 20% of the package cost will apply.
  • Extra Charge will be added if time limit is exceeded.
